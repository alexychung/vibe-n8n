# Weekly News Roundup

Every Monday morning, pull the latest posts from a few RSS feeds (TechCrunch,
Hacker News front page, The Verge). Grab the title and link from each one,
mash them together into a single list, and send it to me as a webhook payload
I can pipe into my email.

Keep it simple. I don't need AI summaries or anything fancy. Just titles and
links, grouped by source.
